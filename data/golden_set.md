# Golden Set (v1)

Purpose:
Evaluate whether semantic similarity captures structural and vibe-level jam similarity,
not just era or song-name clustering.

---

## ghost_1997-11-17

Primary Similarity Axis:
Cow funk groove pocket → minor tonal drift → patient ambient dissolve

Why This Is an Anchor:
This jam represents late-97 groove-based improvisation with subtle exploration.
Similarity should emphasize groove architecture and textural evolution.

Expected Similarity Should Favor:
- Groove-driven jams
- Cow funk / pocket-based interplay
- Late 1997–1998 stylistic feel (but not strictly same era)
- Patient builds rather than immediate peaks
- Textural layering over melodic climax

Acceptable Matches:
- Tube (late 97)
- Wolfman’s (97–98 groove versions)
- 2001 groove-driven jams
- Certain Sand versions (if groove-first)
- Some ambient-groove hybrids

Should NOT Match:
- Machine-gun shred peaks (mid-90s Reba/Maze style)
- Bliss-heavy major-key peak jams (e.g., Alpine Ruby Waves)
- Dark dissonant tension jams (94 Bowie, SOAM)
- Short composed tension pieces
- Matches based purely on same-song identity

Failure Modes To Watch For:
- Returns mostly other Ghosts regardless of vibe
- Clusters strictly by era (all Fall 97 no matter the style)
- Overweights “funk” and pulls in unrelated dance grooves
